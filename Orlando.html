Orlando

"The City Beutiful"

    Fun Facts

  • Home to more than 100 lakes.
  • Until the 1800s was the main hub of Florida's citrus industry
  • Originally named Jernigan.
  • Rogers Building and the 1890 Railroad Depot are the oldest remaining structures
  • 66 million people visited Orlando in 2015
  • Nicknames: "The City Beutiful" or "O-Town"
  • Gatorland is Orlando's first ever theme park, opened 1949.
  • Population of 307,573
  • Incorporated Feb. 4 , 1885

Magic Kingdom

Visiting Disney World's Magic Kingdom is like stepping into a storybook come to life. As you cross under the iconic train station and catch sight of Cinderella Castle, an air of enchantment envelops you. The vibrant colors, cheerful music, and the palpable excitement in the air make it a place where whimsy reigns supreme.
